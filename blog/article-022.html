MACD用的人多，用对的少。标准MACD（12,26,9）在震荡行情里容易来回假信号。我测试了多周期共振的改进方案，效果好很多。

标准MACD

import talib; macd,signal,hist = talib.MACD(close,fastperiod=12,slowperiod=26,signalperiod=9)。标准用法MACD上穿信号线买入，下穿卖出。问题是MACD本质上是均线的均线，滞后严重。

多周期共振

核心思路：日线MACD方向 + 4小时确认 + 1小时入场点：def multi_timeframe_macd(daily_close,h4_close,h1_close): daily_macd,daily_signal,_ = talib.MACD(daily_close); daily_trend = daily_macd > daily_signal; h4_macd,h4_signal,_ = talib.MACD(h4_close); h4_confirm = (h4_macd > h4_signal) == daily_trend; h1_macd,h1_signal,h1_hist = talib.MACD(h1_close); entry = (h1_macd > h1_signal) & (h1_macd.shift(1) <= h1_signal.shift(1)); return daily_trend & h4_confirm & entry
